FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What technologies do you use for web development?

We specialize in modern stacks: React, Next.js, Node.js, Python, and cloud platforms like AWS. For AI integration we leverage TensorFlow, LangChain, and vector databases.

How do you implement AI in your solutions?

We build custom AI agents, RAG pipelines, and fine-tuned LLMs for automation, customer support, and data analysis. Our expertise includes OpenAI, Claude, and open-source models.

Do you offer cloud infrastructure consulting?

Absolutely. We design scalable, secure cloud architectures on AWS, Google Cloud, and Azure. From serverless to Kubernetes, we optimize for performance and cost.

What's your approach to data security?

We follow zero-trust principles, end-to-end encryption, and regular audits. GDPR and SOC2 compliance are baked into our development process.

How do you measure digital marketing ROI?

We use multi-touch attribution, custom dashboards, and predictive analytics. Every campaign is tracked against KPIs like CAC, LTV, and conversion lift.

What's your typical project timeline?

Simple websites take 3-5 days, complex AI applications 2-4 weeks, and ongoing marketing campaigns are continuous. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.
